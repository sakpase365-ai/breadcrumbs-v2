# PRODUCT_CONSTITUTION.md

## Core Wedge

Breadcrumbs is private family intelligence.
It captures parent voice and memory in a reusable way so loved ones can receive the right message later.

## Non-Goals

- Not social
- Not public content
- Not generic journaling
- Not prompt-first writing

## Capture Contract

1. First interaction is action-first: **Write** or **Record Audio**.
2. Prompt suggestions are assistive, secondary, and shown below active capture.
3. Recipient prompt is explicit: **"Who are you speaking to today?"**
4. Recipient is not implicitly selected when options exist.
5. Classification happens after content creation: **Message / Memory / Lesson**.
6. Save is blocked until minimum capture contract is satisfied:
   - content exists
   - recipient chosen (if family members exist)
   - classification chosen

## Family Agent Contract

- Answers should sound like the family speaker, in first-person voice.
- Answers are grounded in Family Foundation + Breadcrumbs + family context.
- When confidence is weak, agent should avoid over-claiming.

## Experience Tone

- Quiet, warm, direct
- Human-first, non-corporate language
- Minimal friction around capture and recall
